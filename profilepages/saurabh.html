Saurabh Sharma

Director at Uniment Solutions LLP

Early Career and Sales Expertise

Saurabh Sharma is a seasoned professional with over a decade of experience in sales and business management. He began his career at Reliance, where he spent 10 years developing expertise in client acquisition, relationship management, and sales leadership. His ability to lead large teams, consistently meet sales targets, and build lasting relationships with clients positioned him as a top performer in the sales industry.

His experience at Reliance equipped him with strategic thinking, allowing him to identify opportunities for growth even in challenging environments. His critical decision-making and ability to understand market trends have been key to his leadership roles today.

Transition to Uniment Solutions LLP

In 2024, Saurabh co-founded Uniment Solutions LLP, a digital marketing firm based in Jaipur, India. As a 50% Director, he has played a crucial role in steering the company’s vision and strategic direction. His deep understanding of sales, combined with his leadership acumen, has enabled Uniment Solutions to grow its client base and reputation in the digital marketing industry.

Saurabh has helped the company design customer-centric solutions that align with market trends. His ability to build long-term client relationships has been instrumental in the company's growth and success.
